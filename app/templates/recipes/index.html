Paneer Butter Masala

Rich, creamy tomato-based curry with soft paneer cubes. Serves 3-4.

Ingredients
  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 3 large tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp butter, 1 tbsp cream
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste, spices
Procedure
  1. Sauté onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add tomato puree and simmer with spices until oil separates.
  3. Add paneer cubes and simmer in buttery tomato gravy.
  4. Finish with cream and garnish with kasuri methi.
Aloo Gobi

Classic dry curry of potatoes and cauliflower. Serves 3.

Ingredients
  • 3 potatoes, cubed
  • 1 small cauliflower, florets
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin, turmeric, garam masala
Procedure
  1. Heat oil and cumin; add onions and sauté.
  2. Add potatoes and cauliflower, stir with spices.
  3. Cover and cook until vegetables are tender.
  4. Finish with garam masala and fresh coriander.
Masala Dosa (Quick)

Crispy crepe stuffed with mildly spiced potato masala. Serves 2.

Ingredients
  • Store-bought dosa batter (or homemade)
  • 3 potatoes, boiled & mashed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Mustard seeds, curry leaves, turmeric
Procedure
  1. Tempering: heat oil, add mustard seeds and curry leaves.
  2. Sauté onions, add mashed potatoes and spices; cook briefly.
  3. Spread batter on hot tawa to make dosa, add potato masala, fold and serve with chutney.
